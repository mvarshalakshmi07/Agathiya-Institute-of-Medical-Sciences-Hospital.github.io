Eligibility

Who can apply

MBBS

Pass in HSC (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, Biology and English, minimum 50% aggregate (40% for reserved categories), and a valid NEET-UG qualifying score.

Nursing, Pharmacy & Allied Health (UG)

Pass in HSC (10+2) with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics, minimum 45% aggregate. NEET qualifying score required for Nursing.

Postgraduate (MD / MS / M.Sc. / Pharm.D)

Relevant bachelor's degree with completed internship, and a valid NEET-PG score where applicable.

Scholarships

Financial assistance

Government Scholarships

First Graduate, BC/MBC, and SC/ST scholarship schemes processed through the Tamil Nadu e-Grants portal.

Merit Scholarships

Fee concessions for students with NEET rank under 5,000, renewable each year on maintaining required academic standing.

Rural Service Bond Scheme

Partial fee waiver for students committing to two years of service at our rural outreach health centres post-graduation.
