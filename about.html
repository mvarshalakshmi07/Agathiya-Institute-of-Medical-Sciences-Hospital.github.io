About The Institution

Twenty-seven years of training healers in Chennai.

From a single 100-bed teaching hospital in 1998 to a four-school, 2,800-student institution today — built one accreditation cycle at a time.

Our History

From one ward to a teaching hospital

Agathiya Institute of Medical Sciences and Hospital was founded in 1998 by the Agathiya Society for Wellness, Integrity & Nurture, opening with a single 100-bed hospital and an MBBS program admitting 100 students a year.

Over the following two decades, the institute added Nursing, Pharmacy, and Allied Health Sciences schools, expanded the teaching hospital to 750 beds, and became affiliated with the Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University.

Today the institute runs undergraduate and postgraduate programs across all four schools, with an emphasis on early clinical exposure and a hospital that functions as a genuine teaching environment, not a simulation.

1998 Institute founded with a 100-bed hospital and MBBS program
2004 School of Nursing and School of Pharmacy established
2009 Teaching hospital expanded to 400 beds; first NMC recognition
2015 School of Allied Health Sciences launched
2019 NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation; hospital reaches 750 beds
2026 2,800+ students enrolled across four schools
Vision

To be recognised as a centre of medical education that produces clinicians capable of compassionate, evidence-based care from their very first posting.

Mission

To deliver rigorous, patient-centred clinical education; to serve our surrounding community through our teaching hospital and outreach camps; and to support every student's transition from classroom learning to safe clinical practice.

On Campus

Infrastructure

A working hospital and campus, not a brochure campus — built up year by year over twenty-seven years.

Teaching Hospital

750-bed multi-specialty hospital with dedicated student observation areas across all major departments.

Clinical Skills & Simulation Lab

Mannequin-based simulation suites for Nursing and MBBS students to practice procedures before ward postings.

Central Library

Over 38,000 volumes and subscriptions to PubMed, ClinicalKey, and major medical journal databases.

Anatomy & Pathology Labs

Dedicated dissection, histopathology, and microbiology laboratories for pre-clinical years.

Hostels

Separate hostel blocks for men and women with combined capacity for 1,100 residents.
