Academics

Four schools. Two degree tracks. One clinical standard.

Every program runs on a Tamil Nadu Dr. MGR Medical University-aligned curriculum, with early and continuous exposure to our attached teaching hospital.

Schools

Where students specialise

MEDICINE

School of Medicine

MBBS and postgraduate clinical programs, with rotations across all major departments of the teaching hospital.

NURSING

School of Nursing

B.Sc. and M.Sc. Nursing with simulation-lab training preceding every ward posting.

PHARMACY

School of Pharmacy

B.Pharm and Pharm.D programs with a dedicated drug information and clinical pharmacy unit.

ALLIED HEALTH

School of Allied Health Sciences

Medical lab technology, radiography, and physiotherapy programs with hospital-based clinical training.

PUBLIC HEALTH

Department of Community Medicine

Runs the institute's rural outreach camps and supports MBBS community postings.

S&H

Basic Sciences

Anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, and pathology coursework supporting all clinical schools.
